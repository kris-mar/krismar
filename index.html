Designer, developer, & artist

Hey there! I’m designer currently based in Orlando, Florida. I specialize in interaction design and I love utilizing my background in STEM, fine arts, and marketing to create well-rounded experiences.

I began my journey as a biomedical student, my entire life planned out to work in conservation genetics. I was working in a research laboratory when I was asked to make my first website for the veterinarian club on campus. Before I knew it, I fell in love! I was enamored with the colors, shapes, and mostly, the fact that good design can bring people together and feel pride in their involvement.

In the 3 years past I’ve had the opportunity to work for small brands and big brands alike and gain experience in many facets of the UX field. My workflow includes heavy use of wireframes, prototypes, frontend development, and the Adobe creative suite.
